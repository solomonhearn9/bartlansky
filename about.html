About the firm

About the firm

We are business people with ground-up business experience which we bring to bear in all of our practice areas. We have a specialized practice which focuses on all issues dealing with real property. We know you have worked hard to acquire your property and we will work just as hard to make sure that you and yours continue to profit from your past work

What we believe

We believe that business is more than a game where the pursuit of winning is the only consideration. Individual business decisions by one group can profoundly negatively impact the lives of others. Wrong-headed actions of local town boards, government personnel or competing private corporations touch our clients in the most personal ways. We take these matters personally. We know that clients need their expenditures to translate into results. We strive to deliver results because for our clients the impacts go beyond mere business.

Contact us
